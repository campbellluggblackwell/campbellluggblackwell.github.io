Nelson, Pa.,

June 15, 1925.

Dear Friend:-

I have neglected to write you to thank you for your gift of the Cousins Correspondence Book. I do not believe you could have chosen one who would enjoy the book more than I do. I have read it with much interest and enjoyment. 

I expect to be in New York City, Wednesday of this week, --- a delegate to the Dairymen's League Convention. The Hotel Pennsylvania is our headquarters.

Again thanking you for your splendid remembrance, I am

Very truly ours,

J. E. HAZLETT.
