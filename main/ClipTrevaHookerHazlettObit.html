A Nov. 1962 news clipping, probably from an Elmira, NY newspaper.

Mrs. Treva H. Hazlett

   Mrs. Treva H. Hazlett of 219 West 14th St., Elmira Heights, died Thursday.

   She is survived by her husband, John H. Hazlett of Elmira Heights; a daughter, Mrs. Wrayburn Rogers of Johnson City; a sister, Mrs. Edward Hills of Johnson City.

   Friends may call at the Ballard and Landgren Funeral Home, Elmira Heights, today from 2 to 4 and 7 to 9 p.m.

Transcribed by Wm. Thompson, Nov. 4, 2014
