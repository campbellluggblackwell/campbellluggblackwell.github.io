Campbells of Nelson, PA

The 40th Annual reunion of the Beechers Island Campbell Clan was held at the I.O.O.F. hall, Nelson, Pa on Aug. 18, 1932 - After a bountiful dinner the meeting was called to order by the President Clyde Campbell. Minutes of the last meeting were read and approved. Officers elected for 1933 were. President T. C. Campbell
Vice President. Clyde Campbell
Treas. Harry Kemp.-
Program. -- Reading Martha Butler
Solo- Edward Monroe - Piano Solo - Francis Hall - Piano Solo - Mary Hall.
Tribute paid to M. B. Seely given by Anna Owlett. Clarinet Solo-Seldon Butler
Talks by Kate Horton and Fannie Monroe.

Edward L. Monroe, Secy.
