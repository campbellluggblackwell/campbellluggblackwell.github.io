CAMPBELLs, LUGGs, & BLACKWELLs of Nelson, PA

A letter of recommendation for singer J. Harry Campbell from English conductor/composer Albert Coates to German composer/conductor Richard Strauss

[Images provided courtesy of J. Harry Campbell's granddaughter, Barbara Righter. Thank you Barbara.]

[Envelope for letter from Coates to Strauss]

[Translation follows]

Beechlawn,

Walton Road,

East Molesly,

Surrey,

England,

27th May 1922

Very highly esteemed Herr Doctor,

May I permit myself to introduce a young American singer Mr. John Campbell. Mr. Campbell has a beautiful lyrical tenor voice and I would be very happy if you would be able to listen to the young artist.

I know how busy you are, but I think that you will enjoy this voice.

Hopefully you are doing pretty well, I was very happy to see you again in New York. Maybe we'll meet again at the Wellington Hotel next winter, I'll go back in December.

Yours faithfully,
  Your devotee,
Albert Coates
