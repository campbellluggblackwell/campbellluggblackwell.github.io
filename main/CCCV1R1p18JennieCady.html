CAMPBELL COUSINS CORRESPONDENCE



Nelson, Pa.
  June 11, 1923.

Dear Cousins:-

This, the first letter of the "Campbell Cousins Correspondence", is just a greeting.

If we all take the interest our secretary has taken, we surely will know more of each other; we are not supposed to know anything about slackers.

Cousin Em and Charlie visited in the Cowanesque Valley the last of May, before Charlie’s trip to California and we enjoyed having them.

I hope all the Cousins can be in Mansfield [PA] with Cousin Mabel and Llewellyn at the Annual Cousins Dinner next fall.

From Cousin,

JENNIE M. CADY

- Report 1 - Page 18 -

(Julia Campbell Family)
