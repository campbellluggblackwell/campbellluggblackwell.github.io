Campbell Cousins Correspondence



F A M I L Y   R E C O R D

OF


JOSEPH and ANN CLINCH


C A M P B E L L

* * *

(Printed October 1, 1922,

and distributed at Cousins

Dinner, October 5, 1922).

* * *

Joseph Campbell, of Elkland Township, Tioga County, Pa., [now Nelson] son of Joseph and Mary Harper Campbell, of Scotch parentage, was born in Ireland December 24th 1793. Ann Clinch, his wife, was born in England January 27, 1804. They were married February 29, 1822, and to them were born twelve children.
