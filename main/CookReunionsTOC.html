Campbells of Nelson, PA

Table of Contents for Cook Reunions

[The Cook Reunions of 1958 - 1970, held in Dennison Park, Corning, NY, were for Campbell Cousins who were descendants of Burton Cook, born 1846 in Nelson, Tioga Co., PA & died there in 1917 and his wife, Mary Jane "Jennie" SWAN Cook, born 1858 in Town of Tuscarora, Steuben Co., NY and died 1930 in Nelson, PA. Burton was a son of James Cook (1804 - 1893) and Rachel Hazlett (1812 - 1893). Rachel was a daughter of John Hazlett and Jane Campbell (1770 - 1832), who was a daughter of Joseph & Mary Harper Campbell.]
