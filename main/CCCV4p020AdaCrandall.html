Wellsboro, Pa.,

March 19, 1926.

Dear Cousins All:-

We ought to be very thankful that we can send greetings to all of the Campbell Cousins. There have been so many of our friends and neighbors called home1 the past winter.

I came to Wellsboro2 on December 12th. It has been a cold winter with a good deal of sickness here. There are many cases of Grippe3, but we hope for warm weather soon and then we will all feel better. I heard some summer birds this morning and it sounded good to me.

I am anticipating with much pleasure in reading the letters.

Sincerely,

COUSIN ADA CRANDALL

1. Died. Gone to Heaven.

2. She was ill and went to her son and daughter-in-law's.

3. Influenza
