Osceola, Pa.,

March 26, 1926.

Dear Cousins:-

We will write a few words to the Cousins this morning, but as we live back on the farm we haven't much of interest to tell.

It has been the longest winter of snow and cold that we have experienced since we lived here, but Spring has come at last, a sugar snow is falling this morning and sap is running All are invited to drop in and have a dish of warm sugar.

  The boys are all in school, Hugh graduates at Elkland this year. He is staying at present with Mother Ann Van Dusen, who has been quite sick. She is gaining nicely now and we hope will soon enjoy good health.

Will close with greetings and best wishes to all the Cousins.

Sincerely,

COUSIN BURR & OLA VAN DUSEN.

Volume IV- Page 28
(Jane Campbell Family)
