Maria B. CAMPBELL Loop (1842 - 1873)

Maria Campbell was born Sep. 5, 1842, in Nelson, Tioga Co., PA; d. Mar. 8, 1873 in Tompkins, Tioga Co. Married Feb. 1, 1862 to James William Loop; b. May. 27, 1842, in PA; d. Aug. 28, 1912 in Elmira, Chemung Co., NY. James' parents were George Loop and Wealthy Philly, both born in NY.

Maria was the 11th of 12 children of Joseph Campbell, b. 1793 in N. Ireland or Scotland, d. 1864 in Nelson; and Ann Clinch, born 1804 in Gloucestershire, England, died 1872 in Nelson.

James and Maria Campbell Loop had two children:

  1. Stella Ann Loop, b. Feb. 13, 1863 in Nelson, PA, m. William E. Hooker, and Nathan B. Wilbur; and
  2. Bertha Loop, b. Oct. 16, 1867, d. Jul. 18, 1883.
