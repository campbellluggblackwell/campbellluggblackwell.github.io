Known Burial Location
from our Campbell/Lugg/Blackwell Tree

PURPOSE

Many of our readers, when traveling (especially to our Campbells of Nelson Reunions) would be interested in visiting the graves of their relatives. To help with this, we have created a list of all our known burials, domestic and foreign. It's sorted by:


  • State (or country)/County/Municipality
  • Cemetery
  • Birth Surname &
  • First Name
It's a work in progress, but includes all known burials.

THE BURIALS LIST
Enjoy the burials list, we hope it's useful.  Additions and corrections are welcome, see our tree for contact info. If a deceased descendant of our Campbells, Luggs or Blackwell's (or their spouse, father-in-law, or mother-in-law) is not shown, please send us their burial info.

VOLUNTEERS
Volunteers are needed to help add the burial lot info. Anyone reading this page has the necessary skills. I'll give you the URLs for looking up the data. I could do it all myself, but I have a lot of other things to do, and this is an easy way for you to help out with this web site. Your assistance will be appreciated and you will be given credit for your help. See our tree for contact info.

Pick the area you'd like to help with.

We also need volunteers to serve as guides for tours of cemeteries near Nelson, PA. Out of the area visitors to our Campbell Reunions often want to visit local cemeteries. Our reunions held in even numbered years, usually in August. Therefore it's not a big demand on your time. Cemeteries visitors want to see include: Nelson Cem.; Lawrenceville's Powers Cem., Elkland's Highland Cem.; Osecola/Fairview Cem.; Knoxville's Riverside Cem.; Farmington Hill Cem.; & Steuben's Baxter Hill Cem.
