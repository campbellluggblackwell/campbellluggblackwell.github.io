Florence H. BOSARD Neal (1887 - 1965)

Florence Hortense Bosard was born Jul. 20, 1887, in Lawrenceville, Tioga Co., PA; d. Oct. 23, 1965, probably in Washington, DC. Married 1925/26 to John Pratt Neal, b. May 1872, in MD. John's parents were John T. Neale, born in PA, and Mary B. ___, born in MD.

Florence was the youngest of 3 children of John Mahlon Bosard, b. 1848 in Osceola, Tioga Co., PA, d. 1889 in Dunkirk, Chautauqua Co., NY; and Jennie Crane, born 1852 in NY, died 1925 in Washington, DC.

John and Florence Bosard Neal had no children.

Florence served as Secretary of the CCC Club in 1924 and Vice-Pres in 1925.
