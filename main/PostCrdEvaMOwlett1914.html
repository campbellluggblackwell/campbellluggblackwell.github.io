Campbells, Luggs & Blackwells of Nelson, PA

1914[?] Postcard of Syracuse, NY to Eva Mattison Owlett. Courtesy of Allen Potter.

[Mailed from Little Marsh, PA. Does anyone know who the sender may be? Presumably a fairly young girl.]

Hello Eva,

How are you? Will hope you are the same. I think you are yellow. You haven't sent that card. How is Addison Hill. Hope to hear from you soon

G E _ [3rd letter very faint]
