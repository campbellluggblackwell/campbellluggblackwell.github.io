2022 Campbells of Nelson Reunion

Our next reunion will be August 6, 2022, via ZOOM. Our 2020 reunion was held via ZOOM for the first time because of the COVID-19 pandemic. We missed the camaraderie of meeting in person, and the pleasure of sharing a pot luck meal. BUT, it provided an opportunity for many to join us for the first time. Cost, travel time, being a caregiver to others, infirmities of age, etc. instantly faded. For instance, a cousin joined us from a remote part of British Columbia who would not have come to Nelson. Perhaps cousins from other countries may decide to participate in the future -- no Visa & passport needed. Nor airfare, hotels, meals, etc. (I met one of our English cousins, Rev. Edward Claude Essex years ago -- it would be great if more, including our English Blackwell cousins can join us.)
