Ann TUBBS Van Dusen (1855 - 1930)

Ann Tubbs was born Jan. 29, 1855, in Elkland, Tioga Co., PA; d. Nov. 15, 1930 in Osceola, Tioga Co., PA. Married Nov. 8, 1878 in Osceola to Oscar Adelbert Van Dusen, b. Jul. 7, 1849, in Farmington Twp., Tioga Co., PA; d. Jun. 29, 1923 in Osceola. Dellie's parents were William Van Dusen, born 1823 in Schoharie Co., NY and Emily Cady, born 1827 in PA.

Ann was the 2nd of 3 children of Jane Campbell, b. 1834 in Nelson, Tioga Co., PA, d. 1916; and George W. Tubbs, born 1829 in Elkland, Tioga Co., PA, died 1916 in Osceola.

Del Van Dusen was a farmer. He first married Martha A. Mowrey, who was a sister of Alfred D. Mowrey, the 1st husband of Cousin Grace CAMPBELL Mowrey Buck Carey. Martha had no children. Del was dying as Vol. 1 Report 1 was written.

Del and Ann Tubbs Van Dusen had four children, all born in Farmington Twp.:
  1. Emma Jane Van Dusen, born Mar. 11, 1870, m. Frank B. Brown;
  2. Harold George Van Dusen, b. Feb. 13, 1882, m. Pearl Celia Place;
  3. William Burr Van Dusen, b. Nov. 27, 1885, m. Ola Davis; and
  4. Ross Van Dusen, b. Nov. 21, 1886, m. Eva Mildred Steadman.
