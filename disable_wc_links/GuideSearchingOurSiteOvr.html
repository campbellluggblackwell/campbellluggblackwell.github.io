Our Shared Tree

Our family history website, and our shared, gigantic family tree seldom has data on living people. But for info on deceased  people, also search in our family tree for: Campbells, Luggs, Blackwells or Other. Our family tree data is now in one gigantic tree, WikiTree, shared with thousands of other genealogist and containing millions of people. The above links will take you to an appropriate starting point within the huge tree. WikiTree has powerful search features and graphic abilities.
