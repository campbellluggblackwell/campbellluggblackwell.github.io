R. D. 4,

Lyndonville, N. Y.,

March 29, 1925.

Dear Cousins:

I have been requested by Cousin Will Selph to write a letter. I am not much of a hand at writing letters, but will try my luck.

I am at present working on a one hundred and fifty acre farm, for elderly people - a man and his wife. I am four miles from Lake Ontario. This is the third season I have been in this section, and I like it very much. It is all level land, mostly fruit.

I was in Pennsylvania for three months this winter. We had some deep snow and some real winter weather.

I am glad to be numbered with the cousins. I enjoy hearing from them.

Sincerely,

COUSIN BURTON OWLETT.
