Jennie Mary SELPH Cady (1873 - 1947)

Jennie was born Oct. 13, 1873 in New Haven, Shiawasee Co., MI; d. 1947.

She was the youngest of 3 children of Julia Campbell, b. 1845, in Nelson, d. 1873 in New Haven, Schiawasee Co., MI; and William Edward "Ed" Selph, Sr., b. 1844 in NY, d. 1875.

She married on Aug. 21, 1891 in Osceola, Tioga Co., PA to James Alfred "Alta" Cady, b. July 9, 1863 in Elkland, Tioga Co., PA, d. 1937. He was a son of Erastus Cady, b. 1833 in PA, and Harriett Peters, b. 1833 in NY.

After her mother's death, Jennie was adopted by her aunt, Jane CAMPBELL Tubbs.

Alta and Jennie Selph Cady had no children.
