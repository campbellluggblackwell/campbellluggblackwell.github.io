MISS AGNES M. HORTON

   Miss Agnes M. Horton aged 71 years of 321 Jefferson Ave., Rochester, N. Y., died at the Christian Science Foundation at Princeton, N. J., on Sunday March 31, 1963. She was a retired Rochester School Teacher, having taught there for 42 years. She was born at West Leroy.

   She is survived by two nephews, Burt of Spencerport, N. Y., and Rex of Albion, N. Y.; one niece, Miss Evelyn Horton and Mrs. Editha Horton, both of Rochester, N.Y.; several cousins including Mrs. Julia Carl of Horseheads, N.Y. and James H. Best of Canton.

   The funeral was held at Holloran Funeral Home, Rochester, on Wednesday at two o'clock. Rev. Robert Volk, officiated. Burial was at Beech Flats Cemetery, Canton.
