Minnie Orcelia TUBBS Clark (1864 - 1944)


[Minnie (standing right), with her sister, Ann TUBBS Van Dusen, and mother, Jane CAMPBELL Tubbs]

Minnie Tubbs was born Oct. 20, 1864, in Osceola, Tioga Co., PA; d. there 1944. Married Sep. 21, 1884 in Nelson to William Henry Clark, b. Feb. 16, 1864, in  Woodhull, Steuben Co., NY; d. Nov. 11, 1944 in Osceola. Will's parents were Eleazor Clark, born 1824 in PA and Julia A. Johnson, born 1831 in PA.

Minnie was the youngest of 3 children of Jane Campbell, b. 1834 in Nelson, Tioga Co., PA, d. 1916; and George W. Tubbs, born 1829 in Elkland, Tioga Co., PA, died 1916 in Osceola.

Will Clark was a carpenter who often worked with Lee Smith Tubbs, Minnie's 2nd cousin. [Lee was also: a brother-in-law of Campbell Cousin Joseph D. Hoyt; brother-in-law of the husband of Campbell Cousin Lena SEELY Goodrich; and grandfather-in-law of Campbell Cousin Ande RICE Tubbs.]

Will and Minnie Tubbs Clark had two children:
  1. unnamed Clark, born 1884, died 1885; and
  2. Wallace Clark, b. Jan. 18, 1886 in Osceola, d. there Sep. 4, 1869.
