Evangaline May HOOKER Hills (1885 - 1971)

Eva Hooker was born Apr. 8, 1885, in Nelson, Tioga Co., PA; d., Sep. 4, 1971 in Johnson City, Broome Co., NY. Married Nov. 11, 1915 to Edward Harrison Hills, b. Sep. 14, 1890, in Wellsville, Allegany Co., NY; d. Apr., 1968 in Johnson City. Ed's parents were Homer June Hills, born 1858 in NY; and Caroline "Lina" A. Fletcher, born 1866 in PA.

Eva was the eldest of 3 children of Stella Ann Loop, b. 1863 in Nelson, Tioga Co., PA, d. 1933 in Elmira, NY; and William E. Hooker, born 1856 in PA, died 1925 in Wellsboro, Tioga Co., PA.

Ed and Eva Hooker Hills one child:

  1. Edward Harrison Hills, Jr. (called "Junior" or "Bill"), b.  1918 in NY, m. Virginia ____.

(Eva was quite a talker, especially when with her sister, Treva HOOKER Hazlett.)
