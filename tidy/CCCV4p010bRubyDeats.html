Osceola, Pa.,

March 14, 1926.

Dear Cousins:-

I have been asked to write a letter to the Campbell Cousins, so I will try to write a few lines.

We have had lots of snow up here on the hill this winter. There are a great many drifts left, some as high as four feet. I enjoyed the Cousins Dinner very much. The only thing I don't like is that it does not come often enough. I think we ought to have a good time like that more than once a year.

Must close for now and will try and do better next time. Love to all.

Sincerely yours,

COUSIN RUBY TUBBS DEATS

Volume IV- Page 10
(Jane Campbell Family)
