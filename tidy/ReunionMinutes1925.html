Campbells of Nelson, PA

Aug 20, 1925

The 33rd Annual Reunion of the Campbell Clan was held on High School grounds at Nelson, Pa Aug. 20, 1925. 90 members were present. Ample dinner the Clan went to the I.O.O.F. Hall following program
