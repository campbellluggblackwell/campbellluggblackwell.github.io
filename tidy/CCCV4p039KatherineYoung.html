Boston, Mass.,

March 30, 1926.

Dear Campbell Cousins:-

I am writing from the Newton Hospital to tell you about Miss Marian Loring Young who was born here on March 20th. She is a plump young lady with feminine dimples an a very complacent disposition. Her brothers were slightly disappointed that she was not a boy but everybody else was well pleased.

I expect to be home in a day or so and shall be glad to have the Cousins Book with its interesting letters.

Very sincerely yours,

COUSIN KATHERINE PRATT YOUNG.

Volume IV - Page 39
(John H. Campbell Family)
