Campbell Cousins Correspondence

Osceola, Pa.

March 30, 1925.

Dear Cousins all:

Here we are writing our annual "cousin's letter". I send to all a greeting. I do not feel I can do better than that this time. I am well as usual, and trying to adjust myself to circumstances. My home was burned March second; but I shall still be found in Osceola. Come and see me.

Sincerely,
COUSIN ADA CRANDALL.

Volume III - Page 2
(Mary Ann Campbell Family)
