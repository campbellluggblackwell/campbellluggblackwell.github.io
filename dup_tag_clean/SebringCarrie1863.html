Carrie SEBRING Campbell (1863 - 1938)

Carrie F. Sebring was born Nov. 27, 1863, in Tyrone, Schuyler Co., NY; d. Jan. 16, 1938, in Elmira, NY. She married Apr. 6, 1882 in Tyrone to Adelbert "Del" Eugene Campbell, b. Dec. 19, 1859 in Farmington Twp., Tioga Co., PA; d. Oct. 28, 1922 in Elmira. Del's parents were John Harper Campbell, born in 1839 in Nelson and Calphurna J. Bottom, b. 1842 in Nelson.

Carrie's parents were Cyrus Sebring, b. 1809 in NY and Almira Fenno, b. 1823 in NY.

Del and Carrie Sebring Campbell had two children, both born in Nelson, Tioga Co., PA:

  1. John Harry Campbell, b. Feb. 15, 1884, m. Catherine DeWitt Chambers; and
  2. William S. Campbell, b. Dec. 16, 1888, m. Winifred Florence Tobey.
