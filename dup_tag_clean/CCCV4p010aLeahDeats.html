Osceola, Pa.,

March 15, 1926.

Dear Cousins:-

As the time has come again for the Cousins Correspondence, I will write a few lines. My family are in the the best of health at present. Claude has been sick with neuritis but is better now.

We have had one of those old fashioned winters here on Briar Hill. There is still good sleighing here, March 15th, and the temperature registers around zero nearly every morning.

I surely enjoyed the Cousins Dinner very much and if at any time you do not have  place for it you are welcome to come here.

With love to all the Cousins, I remain,

Sincerely yours,

COUSIN LEAH TUBBS DEATS

Volume IV- Page 10
(Jane Campbell Family)
