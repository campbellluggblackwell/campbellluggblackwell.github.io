Mini-Bios for CAMPBELLs, LUGGs, & BLACKWELLs of Nelson, PA

Mary HARPER Campbell (1749 - 1844)

Mary was born in Scotland. Her birthplace and parents are unknown. She died Nov. 14th in Nelson, Tioga Co., PA (originally called Beecher's Island). She married Joseph Campbell, born 1748 somewhere in Scotland (parents unknown), died 1824 in Nelson. Mary and Joseph lived about 10 years in North Ireland before coming to America, probably in Sept or Oct. of 1810.

They had 8 children:
  • Sarah "Sally" Campbell, b. 1777 in Scotland or North Ireland, d. aft 1860 in Tioga Co., PA. m. Samuel Hazlett, b. 1780 in Scotland
  • Mary Campbell, b. 1779 in Scotland or N. Ireland, d. 1824 in Lancaster Co., PA, m. John Hazlett, b. 1783 in County Down, N. Ireland (no known relation to Sam)
  • John Campbell, b. 1781 in Scotland or N. Ireland, d. 1866 in Pittsburgh, PA, m. Elizabeth Bell, b. 1785 in Chester Co., PA
  • Elizabeth Campbell, b. 1789 in Scotland or N. Ireland, d. Tioga Co., PA, m. Richard Ellison
  • Jane Campbell, b. 1792 in Scotland or N. Ireland, d. 1832 in Tioga Co., PA, m. John Hazlett, b. 1787 in N. Ireland (brother of Sam)
  • Joseph Campbell, b. 1793 in N. Ireland, d. 1864 in Nelson, m. Ann Clinch, b. 1804 in Avening, Gloucestershire, England
  • William Campbell, b. 1795 in N. Ireland, d. bef. 1810 in N. Ireland
  • James Campbell, b. 1798 in N. Ireland, d. 1865 in Tioga Co., PA, m. Mary Blackwell, b. 1806 in Pine Twp., Lycoming Co., PA (Ann Clinch's half-sister)
