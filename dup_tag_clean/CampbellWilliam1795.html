William Campbell (1795 - bef. 1810)

William was born Jan. 6, 1795, in Scotland or N. Ireland; d. in N. Ireland before 1810. He was the 7th of 8 children of Joseph Campbell, b. 1748, in Scotland, d. 1824 in Nelson, PA, (originally called Beecher's Island); and Mary Harper, b. 1749, in Scotland, d. 1844 in Nelson.

Little is know about William.

Mentioned in Campbell Cousins Correspondence Letter:
v4 p89 Jane CAMPBELL Tubbs - William
