Frances PARKS Campbell (1855 - 1927)

Campbell Cousin Frances "Frank" M. Parks was born Aug. 12, 1855, in NY; d. Sep. 22, 1927 in St. Petersburg, FL. Married Sep. 6, 1871 in Farmington Twp., Tioga Co., PA, her 2nd cousin Thomas Clinch Campbell, b. Mar. 25, 1856, in Farmington Twp.; d. Feb. 1, 1933 in St. Pete. Tommie's parents were William Campbell, b. 1822 in Nelson, Tioga Co., PA, and Helen Peasley, born 1826 in NY.

Frank was the 2nd of 3 children of Benjamin Parks and Mary Campbell.

Tommie and Frank Parks Campbell had two children:

  1. Mary Helena Campbell, b. Nov. 25 1878 in Nelson, m. Omar Curtis Shewman; and
  2. Ruth Campbell, b. Jul. 25, 1895 in Knoxville, m. Merritt Merwin Mitchell and Richard Henry Edward Jones.
