Dear Cousins:-

The above deaths have been reported to me during the past eleven years. This record should be either written in or attached to your Genealogy Chart.

On this date (June 1st, 1935), only seven Third Generation or own Cousins are living, and there are sixty Fourth Generation or second Cousins living.

Trusting the above record may be helpful to you I am,

Very truly yours,

Wm. E. Selph
